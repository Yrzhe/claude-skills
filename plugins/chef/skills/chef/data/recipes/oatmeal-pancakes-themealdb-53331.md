## 步骤
1. Place all the ingredients in the glass and beat.
2. Let the mixture stand for 10 minutes.
3. Grease a hot frying pan with a little butter and pour a little of the mixture.
4. When it starts to bubble on the surface, turn over with a spatula. Cook over medium-low heat so that they do not burn.
5. Finally, add the caramel and strawberries.

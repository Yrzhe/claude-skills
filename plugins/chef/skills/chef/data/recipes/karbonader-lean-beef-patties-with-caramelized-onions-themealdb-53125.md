## 步骤
1. Grate half an onion and set aside. Slice the rest of the onions and fry in butter on low/medium heat until caramelized.
2. ▢
3. In a bowl, mix together ground beef, grated onion, salt, pepper, nutmeg, potato/corn starch, and water.
4. ▢
5. Form into a sausage and cut 6 patties. Using a knife make a light grid pattern in each patty.
6. ▢
7. Brown both sides of the karbonader in butter on high heat, then turn down to low heat and fry for another 2 – 3 minutes.
8. ▢
9. Serve the karbonader and onions with potatoes, stewed peas, and brown sauce for dinner, or on a slice of bread for lunch or a snack.

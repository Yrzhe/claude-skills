## 简介
mom s oven fried chicken just like she used to make it.

## 步骤
1. preheat oven to 350f
2. melt butter in a 9x13 pan
3. mix flour paprika salt and pepper in a shallow dish
4. dip chicken pieces in butter
5. roll in flour mixture
6. place in pan
7. bake 45 minutes turning once

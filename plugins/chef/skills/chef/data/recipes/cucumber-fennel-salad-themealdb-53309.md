## 步骤
1. step 1
2. Put cucumber in a sieve. Sprinkle with 1 tsp salt and the sugar, then leave for 10 mins. Add fennel.
3. step 2
4. Mix soured cream, lemon juice, vinegar and dill, then season with black pepper and add to fennel mix.

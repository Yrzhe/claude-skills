## 简介
creamy cauliflower soup.

## 步骤
1. melt butter in a heavy saucepan
2. add the onions
3. cook covered over medium heat until translucent stirring occasionally
4. add the cauliflower and broth
5. bring to a boil
6. simmer covered until tender about 10 minutes
7. puree the cauliflower mixture in a blender
8. return to saucepan add cream and milk
